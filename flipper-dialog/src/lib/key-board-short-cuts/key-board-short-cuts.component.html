Keyboard Shortcuts

Keyboard shortcut keys Descriptions
Shift + + Increasing a highlighted cart item quantity
Shift + - Decreasing a highlighted cart item quantity.
Shift + S or Shift + F Find or search results (items, products or drugs) in a stock.
Shift Start pressing a key number in the calculator.
Delete Delete a highlighted cart item.
Backspace Clean search box or received cash amount.
Enter or End Collect Cash or Complete an order.
Esc Close.
Shift + K Open Keyboard Shortcuts model
